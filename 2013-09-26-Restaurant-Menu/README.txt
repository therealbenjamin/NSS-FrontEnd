Restaurant Lab
You manage a restaurant.
You need to add items to your menu.
Each item will have the following:
A name
Type of item(appetizers, salads, lunch, dinner, dessert, etc)
A price
Number of calories
List of ingredients
An example:
Spinach and Artichoke dip
Appetizer
$5.50
440 calories
spinach, artichoke, mayo, butter, salt
